Getting Started

Storey runs the same on the phone app and the website — same screens, same login. Use whichever is handy.

Two ways to use Storey

On your phone (App)On a computer (Website)
Install Storey from the Google Play Store. Best for the field — attendance, daily logs, expenses, photos. Open storeyinfra.com in any browser. Best for the office — reports, budgets, team management.

Your data is the same in both. Mark attendance on the phone at site, see the report on the website at the office — instantly.

Signing in

Open the app or website. You land on the sign-in screen.
Choose a method: Google, Email & Password, or Phone OTP.
First time? A contractor registers the company. Team members join with an invite code.

How to approve a Supervisor's expense

Open Expenses from the menu.
Set the Status filter to Pending — these need your decision.
Check the amount, category and note.
Tap Approve (counts in reports) or Reject.
Note: A Site Manager sees only assigned sites — not the whole company. For the company-wide view, that's the Contractor's Reports.

CHAPTER 8

Good Habits & Support

For a smooth site

  • Mark attendance every morning — it's the base for payroll and daily logs.
  • Raise expenses the moment cash is spent — don't wait till evening, things get missed.
  • Site Managers: clear pending expenses daily — keeps reports accurate.
  • Store Keepers: record receipts the same day goods arrive — stock stays trustworthy.
  • Contractors: review the Site Report weekly — catch overspend early.

Privacy & safety

  • Only the last 4 digits of a worker's ID are stored — never the full number.
  • Each company's data is fully separate — no one outside your company can see it.
  • Sign out on shared devices: Settings → Sign out.
