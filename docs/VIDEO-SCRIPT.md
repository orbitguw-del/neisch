## The 75-second script (read aloud, pause where marked)

```
[Hook — 8 seconds]
If you run a construction site, you already know this story.

Attendance is on WhatsApp. Daily logs are on paper. Materials are
in three different sheets. And nobody can find anything two weeks later.

[Problem framing — 12 seconds]
Tally tracks your money. But nothing tracks your operations.
That's where the leaks happen — cement walks off site, sub-contractor
agreements turn into disputes, payments slip.

I'm Karun, and I'm a contractor. I built Storey because nothing existed
that didn't try to make our sites look like a Bangalore office.

[What Storey is — 20 seconds]
Storey is a mobile-first site-operations app for contractors in
Northeast India.

One app on your supervisor's phone runs attendance, daily logs with
date-time-stamped photos, material tracking, tasks, expenses,
equipment issue and return.

Multi-tenant, role-based, offline-ready — built for flaky 4G on
real sites.

[What's coming — 15 seconds]
In the next four weeks: sub-contractor onboarding with one-click
printable Work Orders, material budget vs actual tracking, and daily
backup to your own Google Drive — Tally-style data sovereignty, plus
cloud, plus mobile.

[Trust — 10 seconds]
Twelve contractors are testing Storey right now. Live on Play Store
closed beta. Built solo with AI assistance over the last four months —
171 commits, 30 database migrations, two independent RLS audits.

[Close + CTA — 10 seconds]
Free 30-day trial. WhatsApp me your Gmail at +91 98640 66898 and
I'll add you to the beta today.

It's storeyinfra.com. I'm Karun. Reply on WhatsApp if you have
questions. I read every message.
```

Total: ~75 seconds at normal speech pace.

## Three ways to film this — pick one

### Option A — Selfie video (~20 minutes total)

**Easiest. Most authentic. What I'd recommend.**

1. Open phone front camera, hold steady or rest on a stand
2. Decent light (face the window, not against it)
3. Read the script in chunks — 3 takes max per chunk
4. Don't aim for polish; founder authenticity > production value
5. Trim with WhatsApp's built-in trim or Inshot (free)

### Option B — Screen recording walkthrough (~45 minutes)

**Tools needed:** Loom (free) on laptop OR OBS (free) for screen + camera overlay.

1. Open `storey-beta-invite.jpg` poster as the opening slide
2. Then `storeyinfra.com` on your laptop — walk through:
   - Landing → sign in
   - Contractor dashboard (the new one shipped today)
   - Tap into a site → show workers, materials, tasks
   - Open the supervisor view in incognito → show the visual-first layout
3. Narrate as you click, using the script above as a guide
4. Loom auto-trims and gives you a sharable link

### Option C — PPT-to-video export (~30 minutes)

**Use the deck I just built as the visual track.**

1. Open `C:\consne\storey-pitch-deck.pptx` in PowerPoint
2. Slide Show tab → Record Slide Show → "From Beginning"
3. For each slide, narrate the relevant section of the script above
4. PowerPoint records your voice over each slide
5. File → Export → Create a Video → MP4

The output is a self-advancing video, ~75 seconds, branded slides + your voice.

## What the video should NOT do

- **Don't say "AI-powered."** Storey is built *using* AI but isn't an AI product yet.
- **Don't promise the v1.2 features as live.** Frame them as "coming in 4 weeks" — they ship in mid-June.
- **Don't mention SMS / phone OTP.** That path is in preview; lead with Google sign-in.
- **Don't quote specific final pricing.** Use "free trial · ₹500-5,000 / month / site depending on size" — leaves room for tiering.
- **Don't show fake test data on screen.** If you're screen-recording, sign in as BuildNE (Karun's real test tenant) — the data feels real because it is.

## Recording-day prep checklist

- [ ] Charge phone fully
- [ ] Quiet room (no fan, no AC hum, no traffic outside)
- [ ] Daylight from a window facing you
- [ ] Wear a plain shirt — no logos, no patterns that flicker on camera
- [ ] Read the script out loud 3 times before recording
- [ ] Have water nearby
- [ ] First 3 takes will feel awkward — keep recording, take 5 or 6 will be good

The video doesn't have to be perfect. **It has to be real.** Contractors trust a slightly imperfect founder video more than a polished agency reel.
