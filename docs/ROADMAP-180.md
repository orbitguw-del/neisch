## The 180-day theme

> **From founder-led pilot sales to founder-led repeatable sales.**

90 days got you 5 paying customers — each one a manual, high-touch sale.
180 days is about turning *that* into a system: a referral engine, a CA
introduction channel, and a feature set that doesn't need you to demo it.

---

DAY 120  →  10 paying customers · ₹15-30k MRR · first multi-site customer

**v1.3 candidates to evaluate at Day 100, pick ONE:**
- Project timeline (read-only Gantt)
- Material master catalogue (consistent material names across sites)
- Voice input for daily logs (Hindi/Assamese speech-to-text)
- Notifications (in-app centre + WhatsApp summary)
- Stock-adjustment approval workflow

**Don't pick more than one.** Two half-built features beats neither but loses to one finished feature.

## What NOT to do in the next 90 days

- ❌ **Don't raise.** Even if an angel shows up with a ₹20L cheque. Your terms get worse the earlier you raise. Build MRR first.
- ❌ **Don't hire engineers.** First hire is **customer success / onboarding** — the lane where you're the bottleneck.
- ❌ **Don't pivot.** No matter how shiny another vertical looks. Storey is ConTech, NE-India SMB, Tally-killer wedge. Stay.
- ❌ **Don't build self-hosted / enterprise.** It's a multi-month engineering trap dressed as opportunity.
- ❌ **Don't accept advisor candidates who want >0.5% or won't sign a FAST agreement.** A famous name who never replies is worse than an unknown name who does.
- ❌ **Don't open `ROADMAP-365.md` yet.** Two milestones away. Stay here.
