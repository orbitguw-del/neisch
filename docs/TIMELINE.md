## Weekly review prompts (use these verbatim)

Every Monday at 9 AM, paste these into a session and answer honestly:

1. How many site visits actually happened last week? (Count, not estimate.)
2. Did Arun get a Friday demo last Friday? If not, why?
3. How many paying customers as of today?
4. What did I build last week that nobody asked for?
5. What did I defer last week that a real customer asked for?
6. Is my next 5 days primarily: building / selling / planning / other?

If the honest answer to (6) is "building" or "planning" for the second
Monday in a row, the trap is repeating. Reset.
